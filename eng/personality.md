# Personality words

(Not) basic adjectives
-
- **bright** = clever
- **conscientious** = wishing kindness
- **determined** = knowing what to to
- **gentle** = having or showing a mild, kind, or tender temperament or character
- **resourceful** = full of energy
- **sarcastic** = showing sacrasm to others
- **self-sufficent** = basicly, needs no help
- **spontaneous** = chaotic in actions and planning
- **steady** = can handle survive problems them easily
- **straigntforward** = telling what he/she thinks, not keeping secrets
- **sympathetic** = showing sympathy
- **thorough** = paying attention to details

Collocations
-
- **tend to** avoid conflicts = have a *tendention*, trying to always avoid smth
- **on the surface** + **deep down** = like book's cover and book's content
- **a bit too** trusting = too often trust people
- **on the whole** all is ok, but usually ... = for the first glance
- **come accross** as - makes first impresion like hhe is like ...

Idioms
-

- this girl is a **heart of gold**
- this redneck is too **down to earth**
- phrenetic (maniac) is **a cold fish** when he kills
- this dirt on my wallpapers is a **real pain in the neck**
- she's such a **soft touch**
- having a **quick temper** usually means you're easily switching emotions
- being **full of yourself** means you're an egoistic person
- **don't hurt a fly** - means you are harmless
- never **lifts a finger** - tends to avoid doing anything
- **the life and the soul of party** - understandable...
