# Conflict and war

People
-

- **ally** = this one (country, man, nation) who decided to help
- **casualties** = those, who were injured during the war
- **civilians** = those who do not participate in battles = гражданские (всп. госслужащие)
- **commander** = person in charge of a group of soliders
- **forces** = army, weapons, navy (флот)
- **refugees** = civilians, who decided (or were forced) to leave country because of war
- **snipers** = soliders, who use long distant weapons
- **survivors** = who survived a war
- **troops** = group of soliders
- **the wounded** (wound = ранение) = who was injured by weapon

Fight, battle
-

- **ceasefire** = temporary peace between 2 armies = перемирие
- **coup** = a sudden change of government (usually violent) = госпереворот
- **siege** = trying to gain control over a city or castle by surrounding and stopping the food supply = осада
- **civil war** = war between civilians of the same country = гражданская война
- **treaty** = formal agreement between two countries = соглашение
- **revolution** = an attempt to change government by a large group of people
- **rebelion** = an attempt to change a government by a small group of people violently

other

- **rebel** = who participates in rebelion
- **break out** = quickly started
- **owerthrew** = forcefully remove the government = свергнуть
- **to shell** = to fire missilies = обстреливать
- **to retreat** = move back = сдаваться
- **surrender** = to give back, admit you have lost = сдаться
- **blow up** = to do a boom = взорвать
- **declare** (a war) = officially declare that war has started
- **to loot** = to rob and steal things from where civilians were removed = мородерствовать и грабить
- **to capture** = take them in prison = захватить в плен
- **defeat** = to beat, they couldn't defend = разгромить
- **release** = to set free = освободить
- **to execute** = to punish by killing = казнить
